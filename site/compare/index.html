Keep identities stable

Use ErrorIdentity.code as the external identity, while numeric ErrorCode remains compatibility-oriented.

Separate semantics from routing

source_err creates entry or boundary meaning. conv_err remaps reason only.

Export deliberately

Use reports, stable snapshots, and exposure snapshots instead of leaking internal error shape directly.

Stay compatible

Interop APIs keep standard-error ecosystem boundaries explicit when a caller needs std::error::Error.
